Brightspot Virtual City
Project Type
3D Virtual City
Project Year
2021

Brightspot is a 3D virtual city where people can attend the event virtually.

Brightspot Events wanted to transform their annual city-based event into an online experience due to the pandemic restrictions. They wanted to replicate the excitement, engagement, and sense of community found in a real-life event, while ensuring a user-friendly experience for attendees of all ages and technical abilities.

I was tasked to create design and develop a custom virtual city platform that captured the essence of the real-life event. Not only a virtual city, i also created 3Dinteractive venues and 3D booth design where people can buy and sell things.
