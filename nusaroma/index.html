Nusaroma Kids - Label Design
Project Type
Graphic Design, 3D Character Design
Project Year
2022

Nusaroma is a local essential oil brand from Indonesia that used natural ingredients for its products.

I had an opportunity to create new label designs and 3D characters for one of the sub-products, Nusaroma Kids. The main goal is to bring joy and fun impression by creating a playful design of label & character combined with a vibrant color palette while maintaining the modern design approach so that it can be more appealing to children and parents.
