Harfieyuda is an Indonesia based designer who is working on daily graphic design, user interface, motion design, and 3D design. He is currently working as an Art Director at Duality Studio.

Harfieyuda finished his undergraduate in Visual Communication Design at Bandung Institute of Technology in 2020 and started his career as a graphic designer at the same year. He tackles many visual mediums for different kind of project to diverse his skillset and philosophy as a designer.

Outside of work, he is just busy running outside around the city for his Strava.
